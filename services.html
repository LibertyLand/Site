Services

The administration offers different services of things that
can't be done with Creative or they would take too much time.
Services must be paid in tokens to the administrator doing the task.

Hologram

Put a hologram with custom text and floating item!
Fancy way to show text to the players.
Editing has a different cost.
Only available for Town mayors.

Cost: 2 Tokens

Editing: $20,000

TP Block

Setup a command block with a TP command.
Useful to teleport residents to different places of the Town.
Editing has a different cost.
Only available for Town mayors. They must be for public usage (Resident and Visitor).

Cost: 4 Tokens

Editing: $20,000

Portal

Portals can be installed and setup to teleport to other dimensions (Nether and End), the Spawn,
Business, Events and Resources worlds.
Only available for Town mayors. They must be for public usage (Resident and Visitor).

Cost: 4 Tokens

Spawner

Useful to create special spawner rooms for mobs.
Buy a spawner for whatever mob you want (with some exceptions)
and place it on your town.
Remember to ask an Administrator to protect the zone especially to prevent accidental destruction!

Cost: 4 Tokens

Schematic

Paste your creative buildings on the server.
You can also move buildings inside the server.
Minimum prize is 1 Token/plot but may vary depending on materials used.

Cost: 1 Token/plot

Zero layer

Empty a plot to layer zero in less than 5 seconds!

Cost: 1 Token/plot
