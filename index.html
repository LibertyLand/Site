Towny

Our main gamemode is Towny, where you can build your own town, or participate on another one.
Build an unique construction, or create a money empire in our Business world.
Make epic PVP battles in the Resources world, or marry a player and live the love!

Custom builds

All our buildings are made by us. Originallity and creativity at its finest!
Our building team is a dedicated one, with members that love to build and show their buildings to other people!

OP Tools

Competent Staff

Our Staff team has experience moderating and administrating other servers and services.
Our Staff selection proccess is very strict, and we only choose people who deserves it.
You can trust them on anything you want, be assured your secret won't be leaked.

Version Support

Our Network fully supports the newest and more popular Minecraft versions.
From 1.8 to 1.13.2, we don't limit anyone who wants to play in our server.
We aim to update to the latest Minecraft version as soon its possible, however, we usually wait a while to test and ensure Network stability is good enough.
