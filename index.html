A New server and a new experience

We work constantly on the server to keep our users happy. Most of our custom projects and forks are Open Source, so anyone can contribute. We take in account all of the feedback we get from our users. The donations for our server go directly to pay hosting.

Towny

Our main gamemode is Towny - a town-like mode where you can create your own town, join someone else's and start wars!
Build a fancy tower to scare off your enemies, a top-secret villain meeting point or simply your own, personal base to snuggle.
Start epic PVP battles in the resources world, or marry another player and live the love!

KitBattle

Ready to live amazing fights in the battlefield? Jump in into the KitBattle server and rank up by obtaining XP.
Try the different kits and buy new ones with abilities and custom items!

Weekly Events

Whether is Towny or KitBattle, we have fun weekly events to compete against other players or just for fun.
Earn prizes for participating, invite your friends and join the event!

Bedrock Support

Along with the latest version of Java Ediion, we also support Bedrock Edition on its latest version.
Java and Bedrock players can play together in the same server and world.
Manage your town from Minecraft in your phone or join the battle using a controller in supported consoles.
Check out our guide on how to connect.
